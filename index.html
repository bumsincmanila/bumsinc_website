Bayanihan Crowd

Bayanihan Crowd is a CSR initiative of Business Unusual Media Solutions Inc. For many years, the company has had several outreach and gift-giving events that motivated its founders to formalize its charity efforts into a structured program of providing for business opportunities to our less fortunate brethren. We envision the platform of Bayanihan Crowd to embody the spirit of this very unique Filipino trait as we encourage and inspire the members of this group to likewise do the same in their own small ways as everyone brings to the table their individual resources and expertise to hopefully start a movement of paying positivity forward.
