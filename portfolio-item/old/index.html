ABOUT US

We Bring Your Media and Events Requirement to the Next Level

Business Unusual Media Solutions Inc. is a unique and specialized multimedia services organization, with highly experienced and extensively trained professionals teaming together to develop highly innovative, extensive and cost effective multimedia solutions that are value based and globally competitive.

Its different departments and services combined with functional and creative research study, personalized techniques and state-of-the-art technology; and the guarantee of giving close and sincere attention to each detail and individual needs of the client gives it a wide vocabulary and probability for a successful end product that guarantees to bring not just quality alternative and “intelligent” solutions, but also technical precision with excellent finish and aesthetic beauty, catering to all advertising and multimedia needs

Mission

To provide our clients highly innovative, creative, extensive and cost-effective multimedia solutions, designs and materials using state-of-the-art methods and systems.

Vision

To be a world-class total multimedia service atelier at the forefront of the media-based global economy of the future.

Philosophy

Producing results to its highest potential while stimulating the rate of innovation and creativity.

Objective

To provide an integrated, specialized and personalized approach to the development of projects responsive to each individual and unique creative need of our clients and user groups

We are committed to give you highly innovative, extensive and cost - effective multimedia solutions that are value based and globally competitive.

Our Services

Business Unusual Media Solutions Inc. was formed as a comprehensive multi-disciplinary professional practice and corporation offering multimedia solutions and services such as:

Video Production

"Provide innovative, creative, extensive and cost-effective multimedia solutions", this is the mission given to the BUMS Inc team in every project. Assuring each clients audio visual requirements are not only given quality recording but are also exciting and out of the ordinary."

Event Coverage

BUMS Inc gives client solutions to uniquely handle and record personal and corporate events providing quality photo and video that are both visually dynamic and extraordinary. You are assured we’ve got you covered with an output that is nothing less than excellence.

Events Management

Partnered with other great companies and individuals that provide solutions to your event needs, we bring passion and enthusiasm to every event, from planning, organizing, coordination and its management. Ensuring memorable experience with all our clients.

Other Services

As a dynamic and diversified firm , Business Unusual Media Solutions Inc. is not limited to the mentioned services it also offers other services that will surely cater your event and media needs which includes: Web Development, Photo booth and Mobile Bar.
